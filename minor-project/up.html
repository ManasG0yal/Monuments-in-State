1. Varanasi Ghats
Ghats in Varanasi are considered as one of the holiest places in Varanasi, on the banks of the Ganges. There are 87 ghats in Varanasi, most of them are bathing and puja ceremony ghats and few are cremation sites.

2.Sarnath Stupa
Sarnath city is located near Varanasi and an important pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Most famous ancient buildings and structures at Sarnath are Dhamek Stupa,Ashoka Pillar and Mulagandhakuti Vihara.

3.Deogarh monuments
Deogarh village is located on the right bank of Betwa River and known for many ancient monuments from Gupta Empire. The Deogarh monuments are protected by the Archaeological Survey of India, such as Shantinath Temple,Dashavatara temple Deogarh fort and Varaha temple.
