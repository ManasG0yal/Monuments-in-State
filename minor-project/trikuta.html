Minimum two hours required
Plan for a minimum of two hours to visit this place. You will have to climb about 60 steps to reach the Ropeway station. The fare was rs.130 per head at the time of my visit. It takes nearly 10 mts to reach the top and it is moving very slow as it is a steep peak. After getting down you are surrounded by guides who demand between 100 to 200 depending on the crowd. It is better to have a guide as they take us faster. You will have to climb rocks to reach the hanuman mandir. It is said that hanuman worshipped three hillock as Shiva vishnu and brahma . You can also see a deep cave where Ramanan is said to have hidden to escape from SANI Balaban and did Thapas.
Overall the place is worth visiting if you can climb small rocks. Old people can avoid. You get lot of kitchen appliances made of iron at cheap rates.

Lots of monkeys are here, any food stuffs should be kept carefully from them. Local administration should put up Danger Boards and Safety Barricade over the hill.
